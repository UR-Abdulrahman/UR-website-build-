The standard approach to testing gym offers: pick one, run it for 7–10 days, spend $200–$400, and hope it works. If it doesn't, you start over. That's $200–$400 of data per test — and you're still guessing.

We built a completely different system. We call it sprint testing, and it generates 10–15× more testing data per dollar than any other method we've seen.

Here's exactly how it works.

## The Core Idea: Traffic Volume Over Conversion Depth

Most agencies test at the conversion level — they measure cost per lead. This requires significant time and budget before you have statistically meaningful data.

We test at the traffic level — we measure click-through rate. CTR data emerges in 48–72 hours with minimal spend.

The hypothesis: **the offer that generates the highest click-through rate from a cold audience will generate the most leads when converted into a full campaign.**

Our data across 100+ studios confirms this holds true consistently.

## Step-by-Step: How Sprint Testing Works

**Step 1: Generate 15–20 Offer Variations**

We brainstorm completely different offers — not variations on the same offer. Different angles entirely:
- "7-Day Free Trial" vs. "First Week $1" vs. "Free Intro Class" vs. "21-Day Transformation Challenge"
- Different hooks targeting different avatars
- Different pain points, different aspirations, different social proof angles

**Step 2: Create Color Block Creatives**

Instead of investing in professional photography or video for each variant, we build simple high-contrast color block images in Canva. Bold text, bright background, one clear message. These perform surprisingly well for traffic testing — and cost almost nothing to produce.

**Step 3: Launch a Single Facebook Traffic Campaign**

All 20 variations go into one traffic campaign in one ad set. Daily budget: $20–$30. This is the entire test budget.

**Step 4: Set Automated Shutoff Rules**

Each ad automatically shuts off when:
- It reaches 400 impressions (enough data to judge CTR)
- Its cost-per-click exceeds $1.50 (below this threshold is noise)

The algorithm distributes budget across all ads as they compete for impressions. Losers get cut automatically. Winners stay live.

**Step 5: Identify the Winner and Scale**

Within 48–72 hours, one or two ads clearly outperform the rest. The winner — identified by highest click-through rate — gets converted into a full lead generation campaign with proper landing pages, follow-up sequences, and conversion tracking.

## Why This Works Better Than Traditional Testing

| Method | Cost Per Test | Time Per Test | Data Quality |
|--------|--------------|---------------|--------------|
| Traditional | $200–$400 | 7–10 days | 1 data point |
| Sprint Testing | ~$30 total | 48–72 hours | 15–20 data points |

Traditional testing forces you to commit to an offer before you have data. Sprint testing gives you data before you commit budget.
